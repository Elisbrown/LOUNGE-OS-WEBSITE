  • Recipe-Level Depletion: Selling a burger deducts the bun, patty, and cheese from stock.

  • Supplier Management: Track purchase orders and vendor pricing history.
  • Multi-Location Sync: Centralized warehouse tracking across multiple restaurant branches.
Security & Administration
  • Role-Based Access: Restrict voids, refunds, and sensitive data to managers only.
  • 100% Offline Architecture: Continue operating flawlessly during internet outages.
  • Anti-Theft Audit Logs: Detailed tracking of every deleted item, void, and drawer pop.
  • Employee Time Tracking: Integrated clock-in/out functionality for payroll reporting.
  • Staff Analytics: Track individual server sales velocity and tip generation.
Reporting & Analytics
  • Comprehensive Z-Reports: Blind-close registers with accurate end-of-day summaries.
